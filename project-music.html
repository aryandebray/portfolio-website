Box 1 — Problem Statement
The Problem
Creating original music traditionally requires years of training or expensive software. Most people cannot express a musical idea directly — there's no bridge between imagination and audio.

The goal: build a system where anyone can describe a sound in plain language and receive a fully synthesised, original audio clip within seconds.

Box 2 — Engineering Approach
The Build
Integrated Meta's MusicGen LLM via the Audiocraft library. The pipeline: text prompt → MusicGen transformer → waveform synthesis → Streamlit playback. Prompt engineering was used to coax specific moods, genres, and instrumentation from the model.
